## About the Project

The **Alpa Landing Page** is the digital entry point to the Alpa payment platform. The project was designed to convey trust, modernity, and robustness—essential characteristics for the financial sector.

With a "mobile-first" and fully responsive design, the page clearly and engagingly presents the company’s transparent checkout services, payment splitting, and financial management solutions.

## Features and Sections

- **Hero Section**: Immediate conversion with clear value propositions.
- **Partners**: Social proof through the integration of client logos.
- **Interactive Calculators**: Real-time simulation of fees and costs..
- **Comparisons**: Benefit cards comparing “Why Alpa” versus competitors.
- **Interactive FAQ**: Frequently asked questions section with smooth animations.

## Tech Stack

The project leverages the most modern tools in the React ecosystem

- **Next.js 15 (App Router)**: Optimized SEO and server-side performance.
- **Tailwind CSS v4**: Utility-first styling with the new v4 engine for instant builds.
- **Framer Motion**: Entry animations and complex interactions.
- **Radix UI**: Accessible base components (WCAG 2.1).
- **Zod & React Hook Form**: Robust validation for contact and lead forms.

## Performance and Results

The site achieves top scores on Google Lighthouse (Performance, Accessibility, SEO), ensuring instant loading even on 3G/4G mobile networks crucial for lead conversion in the Brazilian market.
